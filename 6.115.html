6.115 Microcomputer Project Laboratory: Microcontroller Cart

spring '18

technical skills: Assembly, PSoC, embedded systems

In 6.115 my final project was a cart that could be "programmed" to follow a path. After inputting directions on a keypad to the 8051, the 8051 would relay the directions to the PSoC, which would drive the two motors on the cart. Additionally the PSoC was able to use an encoder paired with a LED and phototransistor to follow finer directions (i.e. only a quarter turn of a wheel).
